About Me

Hello, my name is Christopher Shane Brown. I know you are wondering if I go by Christopher or Chris, but I actually go by Shane. My parents didn't that Shane Christopher Brown flowed well, but always intended on calling me Shane. I was born in Texas, but my family lived in East New Mexico at the time. The best and closest hospital, however, was in Levelland Texas. I have lived in different parts of Texas and New Mexico. In 1996 I graduated from Athens High School and started studying at the University of Texas where I graduated with a bachelors degree in Economics.

It took me a while and several different jobs, but I finally broke into finance in 2009. I spent the next 10 years as a financial analyst. Now you may be wondering why would he leave such and illustrious and exciting career. Well, the answer is two layoffs in three years and not a lot of options, ontop of the fact that I didn't really like it. I did always like programming and such so full stack web development called to me.

Along with all the fun and exciting stuff you just read, I do have some hobbies. I like woodworking and would make that my career if possible. Most of the furniture in my house are things I've built. I also practiced martial arts for over a decade and had my own school charter at one time. Lastly I enjoy reading and writing and have published fantasy novel.
